OUR MISSION

EACH SMILE CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE


It's difficult to bring a smile to someone face, especially when a person is carrying the burden of his family on his shoulder.
We are very thankful to God that we were born in a family which gave us proper education, food & shelter. We were taught that if women are educated, the whole family is educated but this saying does not remain same for each and every family.


In India, there are many families who are not financially sound to support the education of a girl child rather we can say families are not financially sound to educate any child, from a very young age they are forced to work and earn there living, boys are forced to work in a garage, in a hotel or they work as a cheap labour and girls are taught to work as housekeeper.

Sejal an initiative works for bringing 'back' the lost smile on everyone's faces.We believe that "Each Smile can make a difference".We come together to remove the barrier between poverty and happiness, by providing education to underprivileged, food & shelter to the ones in need. We work towards fulfilling the broken dreams of underprivileged children and motivate them in achieving it.
